title: Why This Debate Persists
---

- "Unit test" means different things to different teams (Fowler, 2021)
- Chicago and London answer different questions about behavior
- Mocks are a technique, not a goal; they change what you verify
- The choice influences design seams and refactoring cost
- Senior teams optimize for different feedback loops

Sources: https://martinfowler.com/articles/2021-test-shapes.html, https://martinfowler.com/articles/mocksArentStubs.html
